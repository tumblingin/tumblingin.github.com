Visited <a title="MOCA" href="http://www.mocataipei.org.tw/_english/index.asp" target="_blank">MOCA</a> over the weekend to see _Finding India, new art for the century_ exhibition. It's running till mid of December so plenty of time to visit if you are interested. I'm no arts guy but nonetheless I found it interesting. More details on the exhibition on <a title="finding india fb event" href="http://www.facebook.com/event.php?eid=112089842187956" target="_blank">facebook</a>. Here are couple of pictures I took while I was there. Just couple spoilers out of tens of art pieces on display.
